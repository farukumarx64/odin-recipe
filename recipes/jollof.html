Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof Rice is a famous Nigerian Delicacy.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the rice until it is half done.
  2. Fry the vegtables for a minute or two.
  3. Add the rice and water to the vegtables while they're still frying.
  4. Add the seasoning.
  5. VOILA!
