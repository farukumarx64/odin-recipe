Rice and Stew

Description

Rice and Stew is a tasty dish that I eat very often.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the rice and keep it away.
  2. Fry the vegtables for a minute or two.
  3. Add water to vthe egtables while they're still frying.
  4. Add the seasoning.
  5. When the stew is done, pour it on top of white rice.
  6. VOILA!
